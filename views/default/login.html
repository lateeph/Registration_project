CODE SCHOOL

OUR MISSION At CODE SCHOOL, we mentor individuals into professional programmers in an immersive classroom setting. Located in Madina, Accra, we offer a full-time, 12-week Web Development course designed to prepare students for a career in software development.

OUR INSTRUCTORS Our full-time instructors are industry experts and passionate educators who are committed to the success of every single student. Our courses are structured to provide in-person, one-on-one mentorship and guidance.

OUR CURRICULUM Our structured curriculum is designed with input from Upstate employers to give students a full-stack foundation and to prepare them for careers in technology - right here at home or wherever their career path may go.
